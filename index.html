Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you never needed to know about donating to nothing.

Where does my money actually go?

Nowhere meaningful! Your donation goes toward the cost of certificates, plaques, shipping materials, and the continued existence of this website. Any remaining funds contribute to nothing of consequence. We're refreshingly honest about this.

Is this a real charity?

Absolutely not. This is a satirical art project and novelty gift service. Your donation is not tax-deductible and will not help anyone or anything. That's kind of the whole point.

Why would anyone donate to nothing?

Great question! Some do it for the novelty. Some want a funny certificate to frame. Some are making a statement about charity culture. And some people just have a sense of humor about the absurdity of existence.

Will I receive a physical certificate?

If you donate $25 or more, yes! Donations under $25 receive a digital certificate only. Physical items are mailed within 2-4 weeks. We use real paper and everything.

Can I remain anonymous on the Wall of Fame?

Yes! During checkout, you can choose to appear as "Anonymous" or opt out of the Wall of Fame entirely. Your shame‚Äîer, pride‚Äîis safe with us.

What if I want a refund?

You donated to nothing and received nothing of value. The transaction is complete. But seriously, contact us if there's an issue with your order and we'll work something out.

Is this making fun of real charities?

Not at all! We encourage donations to legitimate causes. This is simply a humorous take on donation culture and a way to get a funny novelty item. Please continue supporting real charities that do real good.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! Nothing knows no borders. International shipping may take longer and we can't be responsible for customs delays. The nothing will reach you eventually.
